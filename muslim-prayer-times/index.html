Muslim prayer times

This page shows you the islam pray times according to your country and city . You can either let the application to determine your location or enter your location manually . To enter your location you need to know your coordinates (longitude and latitude ) You can also choose your city from the list below .Please note that we are only listing the principal cities in the world .If your city doesn't appear then you need to let the app determine your coordinates (You need to give the app the permissions to access your location when it asks you for permission ) or just enter your coordinates ,longitude and latitude . The muslim pray times is also available as an Android application and browser extension for Chrome - FireFox and Opera .
